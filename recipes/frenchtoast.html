Recipe for French Toasts

In this recipe you will learn hoy to properly make the best french toasts you've ever seen, or eaten.

Anyway here it goes, yo will need the following ingredients:

  • Bread
  • Milk
  • eggs
  • Vainilla extract
  • Cinnamon
  • Butter
  • Salt
  • Sugar

Steps:

  1. Mix eggs
  2. Add the milk to the eggs, mix
  3. Add a little bit of salt, cinnamon, and a couple spoons of sugar to the previously made mixture, stir vigorously until the cinnamon breaks up significantly
  4. Soak the bread into the mixture
  5. Add butter to a hot pan
  6. Fry both sides of the bread until golden
